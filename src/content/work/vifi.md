Smart contracts at Virtual Finance: pre-launch roadmap,
production readiness, and founder-facing technical guidance.

I reunited with Polygon alum Tony Olendo for pre-launch smart contracts
work. What started as development grew into review and guidance: taking
stock of the existing codebase and the work in flight, and charting the
path — with Tony and the rest of the team — to get ViFi through audit
and to deployment.

This was also my first role after the explosion of agentic coding. That
meant building systems to turn a rapidly shifting landscape into actual
productivity rather than the illusion of it — and working out how humans
should talk to each other about engineering when more and more of the
engineering is executed by their agents.
