Engineering manager for Alchemix v3 — a ground-up new version of the
protocol spanning a core system, a strategy layer, and a governance layer.

I ran the dev process for the smart contract work: defining and
prioritizing tasks, keeping the devs unblocked, and keeping the lines of
communication open between the contract engineers and the rest of the
org. I also handled recruiting — sourcing, interviewing, and hiring to
round out a team built around an engineer already in place.

The back half of the engagement was a gauntlet of security review —
multiple audits and audit competitions across Immunefi, Cantina, and
more. Alongside the lead engineer and the founders, I carried a large
share of the interface work: triaging tickets, answering whitehats and
auditors, coordinating with the firms, and making sure fixes actually
landed.
